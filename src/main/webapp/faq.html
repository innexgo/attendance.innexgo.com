FAQ

What is Innexgo?

Innexgo is a comprehensive attendance-tracking system that can generate specific individual or large scale attendance reports.


Will the system replace manual paper tracking?

Our system can certainly render paper tracking (logs) obsolete. Students would simply scan out of the classroom for any purposes such as bathroom breaks, passes to the administrative offices, or other purposes. Innexgo will render these situations as classroom disruptions where students are pulled from academic learning time. These instances will be available in the reports.


What stops students from taking pictures of their IDs, printing them out, and then giving them to their peers to scan for them?

This is a human-factored issue that cannot be resolved by any attendance system. The situation must be handled by the attendance administrators to enforce legitimacy and consistency with student IDs. What we recommend educators do is to keep an eye on students as they are scanning into class to ensure they are not scanning two IDs.


What physical materials and hardware does your system require?

Our system enables students to scan in and out quickly and efficiently using their own student IDs, which will be equipped with individual RFID stickers. Each room will be equipped with a scanner box near the entrance.


Does this system track the location of students constantly?

No, this system is not a GPS location tracker. All the system consists of is a RFID sticker that can only be activated within two centimeters of the scanner box, which is the only time your location will be logged (when you enter and exit a classroom). Innexgo is committed to respecting the privacy of both students and educators, and as such we do not constantly track the locations of students.
