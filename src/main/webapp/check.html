Visit Google Takeout to download your location history. You can speed up your download by clicking the "Deselect All" button, and then reselecting "Location History". Make sure to click on the "Multiple Formats" button to ensure your data will be in JSON format. Google will email you a zip file containing your data. Once you recieve the email, unzip the attached file and pick Location History.json.
